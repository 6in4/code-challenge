About

Description

Pablo Ruiz Picasso was a Spanish painter and sculptor who spent most of his adult life in France. One of the most influential artists of the 20th century, he is known for co-founding the Cubist movement, ... Wikipedia
Born: October 25, 1881, Málaga, Spain
Died: April 8, 1973 (age 91 years), Mougins, France
Periods: Cubism, Picasso's Blue Period, Surrealism, Modern art · See more
Influenced by: Vincent van Gogh, Paul Cézanne · See more
Full name: Pablo Diego José Francisco de Paula Juan Nepomuceno María de los Remedios Cipriano de la Santísima Trinidad Ruiz y Picasso
Children: Paloma Picasso, Paulo Picasso, Claude Pierre Pablo Picasso, Maya Widmaier-Picasso
Spouse: Jacqueline Roque (m. 1961–1973), Olga Khokhlova (m. 1918–1955)
